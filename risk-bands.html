Description

Each daily risk score (0–1) is placed into one of ten bands spanning 0.1 units (e.g. a score of 0.35 falls in the 0.3 – 0.4 band). The bar height shows how many days Bitcoin spent in that band over the selected date range.

Switch to Percentage to see each band's share of the filtered period. The subtitle always reflects the latest available risk score in the full dataset, regardless of the active date filter.
