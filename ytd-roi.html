Description

Year-to-date ROI is the asset price at any point during the year divided by the price on January 1 of that same year. A value of 1.0 means flat for the year; 0.5 means a 50% drawdown from the Jan 1 open; 2.0 means price doubled.

Each colored line is one calendar year plotted against days of the year, so you can compare seasonal patterns across years. Years that finish below 1.0 were net down for the year; lines that stay above 1.0 reflect bullish years. This chart uses Binance BTC/USDT daily closes from 2018 onward (2017 is omitted because Jan 1 is not in our dataset).
